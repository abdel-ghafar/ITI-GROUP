Cement & Building Materials Leadership

Over the past five years, ITI Group has expanded its footprint in the cement industry by integrating key production assets previously operated by global leaders such as HeidelbergCement and InterCement. This strategic expansion reflects the Group's commitment to strengthening its market position, enhancing production capacity, and driving operational excellence. With a focus on growth and value creation, ITI continues to solidify its role as a major player in the global cement and building materials sector.

Production Footprint

Established in 1987, Amreyah Cement brings together decades of industry experience and advanced technology to deliver high-quality, dependable grey cement. Operating two fully integrated production lines, the company has contributed to some of Egypt's most iconic landmarks, especially in Alexandria. As it continues to adapt to the evolving needs of the construction industry, Amreyah now proudly exports to markets across Africa, Europe, and the United States.

  • 40% ownership
  • One fully integrated grey cement production line
  • Export to African, European, and American markets
